Overall there were 64 authors involved in the writing the papers. One author works for two institues that are not located in the same country, neither the same continent, therefore he was counted twice, which leaves us with a total of 65 authors. The author was Samer Hassan \cite{2016_Jacynycz}, who works in Madrid, Spain and Cambridge, United States. The figure \ref{fig:rq6_nb_authors_continent} shows that 29 authors came from Europe which makes it with 44.6\% the biggest part. Also not negligible are America with 26.2 \% and Asia with 24.6\%.\\
As in figure \ref{fig:rq6_nb_authors_country}, there are some countries that do more research in this area.
\begin{wrapfigure}{l}{0.6\textwidth}
	\centering
	\begin{tikzpicture}[thick,scale=0.8, every node/.style={scale=0.8}]
		\pie[sum=65,text=legend]{13/United States , 13/China , 5/Spain , 4/Germany, 4/France , 4/Italy, 4/United Kingdom, 18/Others} 
	\end{tikzpicture}
	\caption{Number of authors per country} 	
	\label{fig:rq6_nb_authors_country}
\end{wrapfigure}
The United States and China are the leading countries with each 20\%. But also other european countries like Spain, Germany, France, Italy and the United Kingdom research in this area. Nevertheless, 27.7\% of the authors were from other countries. This shows that various researches in different locations are working on this topic, which proves once more its importance. (high diversity)\\
Overall there were 64 different authors, but two of them work for two different institutions, so they were counted twice.
\begin{wrapfigure}{l}{0.6\textwidth}

These authors were Christian Bach \cite{2017_Jaag} who works for the \quoteit{University of Liverpool} and the company \quoteit{Swiss Economics} and Samer Hassan \cite{2016_Jacynycz} who works for the \quoteit{Universidad Complutense de Madrid} and the \quoteit{Harvard University}. Another interesting question is, if the author was from industry or academia. The results show that only 9.1\% of the authors came from the industry and 90.9\% do their research at universities. All publications done in industry were done in partnership with a university, except for one publication (\cite{2017_Tackmann}), which was conducted only by \quoteit{IBM Research}.
